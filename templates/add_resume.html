Set Up AI Auto-Fill — 3 Easy Steps

The AI feature needs a free API key from Anthropic (the company that makes the AI). It takes about 3 minutes to set up and costs almost nothing to use.

1
Get a free API key
Open this link, sign up with your email, then go to API Keys → Create Key
Open console.anthropic.com
2
Paste your key into config.py
Click the button below to open the settings file in Notepad, then replace your-api-key-here with your key and press Ctrl+S to save.
3
Restart the app
Close this browser tab, double-click START_APP.bat on your desktop again, and the AI Auto-Fill will be ready.
Cost: Anthropic gives free credits to start. Parsing one resume costs less than $0.001 — so even 10,000 resumes would cost less than $10.
