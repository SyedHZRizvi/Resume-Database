Privacy Notice — how we handle your information

What we collect: Your name, email address, phone number, LinkedIn profile, and the resume you upload. We may also collect your cover letter and the position you select.

How we use it: Solely to assess your suitability for current and future roles at TransCrypts. Your resume is analysed by an AI system (Anthropic Claude) to extract professional information and categorise your skills automatically. This is a screening aid — all hiring decisions involve human review.

Who processes your data: Authorised TransCrypts HR staff, and the following service providers acting on our behalf: Supabase Inc. (database, USA), Render Inc. (hosting, USA), Anthropic PBC (AI resume parsing, USA), and our email provider. Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States under appropriate contractual protections.

Retention: Applications are retained for up to 2 years from the date of submission or last activity, after which they are anonymised or deleted.

Your rights: You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time. EU residents have additional rights including data portability and the right to object. Email hr@transcrypts.com — we will respond within 30 days.

Questions or complaints: Contact us at hr@transcrypts.com. Canadian residents may also file a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada. EU residents may contact their national data protection authority.
